From Molly's Desk

Why I Created The Woodinville Journal

Woodinville isn't just where I work. It's home.

As a lifelong Woodinville resident and proud Woodinville High School alumna, I've spent years helping local families navigate some of life's biggest financial decisions.

Along the way, I kept hearing the same questions — from neighbors at school pickup, from clients around the kitchen table, from people who deserved straight answers and weren't getting them.

That's why I created The Woodinville Journal. Every issue is designed to help you make informed decisions about your home, your equity, and your future.

  • 🏡

    "Can we afford to move — with rates where they are right now?"

  • 💵

    "Will capital gains taxes affect us if we sell?"

  • 🔨

    "Which home improvements are actually worth making before we list?"

  • 🏛️

    "What do today's housing policy changes mean for our family?"

Bringing National Housing Conversations Home to Woodinville

Through my leadership with Washington REALTORS®, Seattle King County REALTORS®, and the National Association of REALTORS®, I have the opportunity to advocate for homeowners at the local, state, and national levels.

The Journal is how I bring those conversations back to the people they matter most to: the families who call Woodinville home.
